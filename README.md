# E-commerce Sales & Profitability Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce transactional data to identify key drivers of sales and profitability. The analysis focuses on product performance, discount impact, and regional trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
To identify factors influencing revenue and profitability and provide actionable insights for business decision-making.

---

## ❓ Key Questions
- Which sub-categories generate the highest and lowest sales?
- Which sub-categories are most and least profitable?
- How does discount impact profit?
- Which region generates the highest profit?

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Google Sheets (Data Cleaning)
- MySQL (Data Analysis)
- Tableau Public (Data Visualization)

## 📊 Key Insights
- High sales do not always mean high profit
- Higher discounts reduce profitability
- Profit varies across regions and categories
